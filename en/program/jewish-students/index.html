Help the students right now

27 Apr 2020
Photo by Windows

General education and entertainment programs for those who feel young enough to start or continue their studies. The Jewish Community calls for support of a wide range of programs for people who lead an active lifestyle. Non-formal education, a variety of entertainment, exciting travel and other unique projects provide an opportunity to expand your horizons and replenish your knowledge base. It is in our power to help young people choose a suitable education, take part in informal student communication in the future, pass on their experience to future generations.
